## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a passionate **Software Engineering undergraduate at SLIIT**, currently interning at **Techwire Pvt Ltd** — building web and mobile solutions that are fast, scalable, and user-centric.

I'm also responsible for maintaining and enhancing systems at **Norochcholai Power Plant**, where my work has real operational impact. Whether it's architecting a full-stack web app or debugging a critical system, I bring the same level of care and precision to everything I build.

> *"I enjoy solving real problems with code — and I'm always chasing the next challenge that pushes me forward."*

- 🎓 **B.Sc. in Information Technology** — Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT)  
- 🏭 **Software Engineer Intern** — Norochcholai Coal Power Plant & Techwire Pvt Ltd  
- 🌐 Specialising in **Full-Stack Development**, **System Integration**, and **Mobile Apps**  
- ☕ Fuelled by coffee, curiosity, and clean code
